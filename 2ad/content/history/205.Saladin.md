1169-
Saladin lead Sunni Muslims of Syria in an
 invasion of Egypt and ended the Fatimid caliphate. Saladin's
 forces also invaded Jerusalem, Palestine and Tripoli. The fall of
 Jerusalem led to a 'third crusade.'
