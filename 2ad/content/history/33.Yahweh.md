## _history_  Yahweh-
Yahweh is the Hebrew God worshiped exclusively when
the Hebrews became monotheists,   1000 BCE.
  This is the God in
the Old Testament.   The Hebrews believe that if they place their trust
in Yehweh, then they will be provided for.
